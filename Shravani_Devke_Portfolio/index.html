About Me.

I’m driven by the idea of creating experiences that seamlessly connect people and technology. With a background in Computer Science and Business Systems, I bring together analytical thinking and a creative mindset to design solutions that are both functional and visually compelling.

I specialize in crafting user-centered digital experiences, combining aesthetics with functionality through UI/UX design, visual storytelling, and creative direction. My approach is rooted in empathy and a meticulous attention to detail, ensuring every design decision aligns with the user’s needs and goals.

For me, design isn’t just about aesthetics—it’s about solving problems, evoking emotions, and building meaningful connections that leave a lasting impact.
